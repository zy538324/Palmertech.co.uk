About Palmertech

Founded in 2022, Palmertech began with a simple goal: to build websites that worked better for real people. Starting out by creating projects for family and friends, the dedication quickly evolved into a full commitment to mastering modern development practices. Every spare hour and every academic opportunity was invested in learning, building, and refining.

Today, Palmertech has grown into a trusted partner for individuals, startups, and organisations who need more than just “a website.” That journey of curiosity and persistence is still at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

At Palmertech, we believe technology should be more than just functional — it should empower. Our mission is to help businesses and individuals transform ideas into reality through modern, scalable web and mobile solutions that are secure, intuitive, and beautifully designed.
